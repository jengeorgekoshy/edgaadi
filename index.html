Welcome, seekers of knowledge, to the abode of enlightenment! If you came here to find a mentor who can help you with your studies, then you are in the right place. Who are we?

Ed-Gaadi is a company working on the holistic academic development and mentorship program for students in colleges. We seek to guide them on their knowledge seeking journey through their undergraduate days. Ed-Gaadi provides career development courses, counselling, live sessions with subject veterans in addition to the normal college courses.

Ed-Gaadi is a platform OF, BY and FOR the seekers of knowledge. So, be rest assured that you have indeed landed in the right place.

Here we are with open doors, welcoming you into the learning ethos of Ed- Gaadi!

Mission

To provide the best and dedicated educational services to those who seek knowledge in the most lucid form.

Vision

To guide and mentor students by helping them through their studies and career development and enable them to contribute back to the community.

We have ensured to charge only a minimal price from you to experience the Ed-Gaadi learning atmosphere. Yet, it is completely fair of you to ask: if we are so interested in enlightening the community, why can’t we do it pro bono?

Well, here is the thing,

The directors of this company have had long exposure in working with non-profit organizations. And as per their consensus, it is decided that a part of your fees shall be contributed to the welfare of children from the underserved sections of our society and their education. As we always say, by letting others grow, we are growing in the process too.

So, we assure you not to get vexed, instead feel grateful for learning with Ed-Gaadi, for you, the seekers of knowledge, are becoming a part of the greater good!

Many people enquired us about the meaning behind the title Ed-Gaadi.

Ed is the abbreviation for education, as many of you might have deciphered, and Gaadi in Hindi means vehicle. So in short, it translates to the vehicle of education.

Incorporating our motto “Hop onto success” into the title, the whole moniker can be inferred as – an education wagon enabling the seekers of knowledge to hop onto success. Voila!
